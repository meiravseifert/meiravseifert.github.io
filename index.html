About Me

Meirav Seifert is a queer Tel Aviv Yafo-based graphic novelist, poet, and writer. Represented by the Deborah Harris Agency, Meirav is the creator of the graphic novel, Girl Versus and has had their short fiction published in long con magazine, The Science Fiction Tarot, and other publications. Their work has been recognized with the Naomi Handelman Prize, the ABC Tales Cherrypick and Green Book awards, and table//FEAST Literary Magazine’s Fallow Fields Nomination for Poetry. Meirav teaches language and writing skills; they co-managed the long-standing Wondering Writer’s Workshop between 2015 and 2020. They’ve written and translated science, art, and culture articles for Midnight East and The Weizmann Institute of Science’s news and culture magazine. Having been raised bilingual and multinational in the United States and Tel Aviv, Meirav writes in multiple languages about gender, relationships, and mental and physical health. They aspire to bring together people from all walks of life by offering alternative perspectives on social conventions and breaking down taboos.
